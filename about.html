Meet Krishna Priya

Founder & Master Tailor

Krishna Priya, the creative force behind SOOJI_NOOLUM, brings over 15 years of experience in the art of tailoring and garment design. What started as a childhood fascination with fabrics and patterns has blossomed into a thriving business dedicated to creating exceptional custom clothing.

With formal training in fashion design and years of hands-on experience, Krishna Priya has mastered the delicate balance between honoring traditional techniques and embracing contemporary styles. Her keen eye for detail and innate understanding of body types ensure that every piece created is not just beautiful, but perfectly suited to the wearer.

"For me, each client is unique, and every garment we create should reflect their personality and lifestyle. I believe in listening carefully to my clients' needs and translating their vision into reality through meticulous craftsmanship," says Krishna Priya.

Her specialties include bridal wear, traditional Indian attire, contemporary fusion pieces, and custom alterations. She takes pride in working closely with each client, from the initial consultation through fabric selection, fitting sessions, and final delivery.
